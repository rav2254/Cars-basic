Dodge Challenger Demon 170 (2023)

Nickname: The Great Sendoff

Specs:

  • 0-60: 1.66 sec (on E85)

  • Top Speed: 150 mph

  • Weight: 4250 lbs

  • Engine: 6.2ltr Supercharged Hemi V8

Opinion

If there was ever an award show for muscle cars, The Dodge Challenger Demon 170 would sweep the whole night.

With a 6.2ltr supercharged Hemi V8, the Demon 170 outputs 900HP on premium or to tick it up a few notches you can use E85 gas to get a drag ripping 1025HP!!!

In iconic muscle style, the Demon doesn't output these crazy numbers using precision manufacturing or an aerodynamically efficient design but with pure brute force and a lot of tiny explosions.

To top it all off, Dodge offers drag tires as an add-on when you purchase the Demon for single dollar telling the buyers exactly what the car is built for.

The Demon currently holds the record for the fastest accelerating production car in the world. The stock demon is actually banned from the National Hot Rod Association(NHRA) for having a sub 9 second quarter mile without a roll cage and a parachute. Imagine a road legal production car so quick that it cannot participate in the largest drag racing association in the world.

R.I.P Demon you were the best sendoff we could hope for, for such an iconic car.
