Corvette Z06 C8 (2020-present)

Nickname: The American Supercar

Specs:

  • 0-60: 2.6-2.9 sec

  • Top Speed: 195 mph

  • Weight: 3400 lbs

  • Engine: 5.5ltr LT6 V8

Opinion

The Corvette C8 Z06 is is Chevy's answer to European supercars. Starting with the C8, the Corvette elevated it's status from a Muscle car to a supercar at a fraction of the price. With it's mid engine design and a V8 engine revving upto 8600 RPM and outputting 670 HP stock. The Z06 can be seen giving any other supercar a run for it's money on the drag strip. Making it THE choice for anyone looking for a thrill of a supercar at a "budget".
