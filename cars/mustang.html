Mustang GT 5.0 S550 (2015-2023)

Nickname: The Project

Specs:

  • 0-60: 4.6-4.8 sec

  • Top Speed: 155 mph

  • Weight: 3600-3800 lbs

  • Engine: 5.0 Coyote V8

Opinion

The Mustang GT S550 is one of the most versatile cars in the market. It's a perfect fit for a wide range of drivers and enthusiasts:

The daily driver

Despite it's endless possibilities, you can easily keep the car bone stock with a few aesthetic modifications and you got yourself a daily driver which would still be one of the coolest cars on the road. Even at stock, you still have a 5.0ltr Coyote V8 making around 450HP which allows you to rip it whenever you get a chance or you can cruise around town enjoying that V8 thump that everyone loves.

The Drift King

Being a rear wheel drive, the Mustang is the perfect car for drifting with a few modifications. With an upgraded suspension and brand new brakes, you got yourself a donut king. Just make sure to keep yourself away from the crowds.

A Drag star

The Mustang is like a beast waiting to be unleashed. With regulations, software restrictions, naturally aspirated Engine and a heavy body the incredibly capable Coyote V8 can only output 450HP stock(which is still a lot of horses) but with a few mods you can truly turn this muscle into a rocket on wheels. With a supercharger kit, upgraded exhaust, cold air intake and a carbon fibre kit, you can easily dust a porsche 911 or a Lamborghini on the drag strip. Just make sure to upgrade the other parts to be able to handle the 900-1000HP your upgraded engine is going to output
